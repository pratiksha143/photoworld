ABOUT US
ABOUT ME I am a Wedding and Event Photographer in Zurich, Basel and Geneva.
In 1947, I found a job as a fashion photographer in the US. I used 35mm Leica, unconventional at the time. Between 1950 and ’59 I turned my attention to street and wedding photography. This is where i became most famous. The Guggenheim Fellowship allowed me to travel the country, resulting in my most famous work: The Americans.
I worked closely to Walker Evans and became one of the world’s most famous Event photographers. I supplemented my visuals with text, written straight onto the negatives and prints.
